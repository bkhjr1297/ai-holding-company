# Compliant $0 Startup Pathways

Legal constraints:

- No automated/AI/robocall cold calling without prior express consent in Florida.
- Manual, live-person dialing is the compliant cold-call path.
- Business-to-business cold calling is generally legal manually if you follow TCPA, scrub DNC, and place calls during legal hours.
- AI/recorded cold outbound is restricted or illegal without explicit consent.
- Do not rotate numbers, use spoofing, block caller ID, avoid do-not-call requests, or bypass platform rules.

## Compliant Options

### 1. Manual Warm Outreach By Brian (Free)
- Brian dials from existing phone.
- Reads exact script.
- Logs outcome.
- Uses free email follow-up.
- Immediate start, zero dollars.

Legal requirements:
- Call during legal hours only.
- Honor do-not-contact requests.
- Keep short call records of opt-outs.
- Live human voice, not prerecorded or AI-generated voice.

### 2. Live Answering Service Trial (Inbound Support)
This is inbound, not cold outbound.
- ReceptionHQ, PATLive, Map Communications, Posh, Abby Connect offer 7-14 day trials.
- They answer inbound calls professionally for your business.
- Less relevant for building a cold-calling agency, but useful if we shift to inbound receptionist model.

Examples:
- ReceptionHQ: 7 days, no credit card
- PATLive: 14 days
- Posh: 7 days
- Map Communications: free trial available
- Abby Connect: 14 days

### 3. Inbound AI Receptionist (Later)
- Allo, QuoteIQ, and others offer inbound AI answering.
- Inbound is much more compliant than outbound AI.
- Good model: missed-call recovery by having prospects call or submit web forms, then AI follows up on inbound requests.

### 4. Commission Appointment Setters
- You pay only for booked/warm appointments.
- You provide the list and script.
- Setter does the live manual cold calling.
- Examples: Dial A Closer, Upwork cold-call specialists, Reddit call-center hiring posts.

### 5. Hire A Freelance Apprentice Appointment Setter By The Hour (Later)
- Very cheap entry-level labor.
- Pay hourly, pay for performance.
- Not $0, but low risk for early validation.

## Recommended Sequence

Phase A: Brian manual dials
- Start now, zero dollars.
- Use existing phone and free Gmail.
- Validate demand before any paid tool.
- Rule: stop spending when calls/bookings validate ROI.

Phase B: If Phase A shows demand, hire commission setter
- Still low/spend-after-revenue.
- BD or appointment setter becomes the caller.
- Brian handles discovery/close.
- Use Revenue Rescue Desk scripts, CRM, and follow-up system.

Phase C: If volume justifies, add inbound AI or live answering trial
- Use inbound AI receptionist because it is legally cleaner.
- Use live answering for overflow only.

Phase D: Once MRR is stable, upgrade tools
- Domain, scheduling, CRM, ad spend.

## What We Will Not Do
- No AI autodialer cold calling.
- No prerecorded voice cold outreach.
- No DNC list rotation or circumvention.
- No spoofed caller IDs.
- No automated systems placing marketing calls to residential or personal numbers without consent.
