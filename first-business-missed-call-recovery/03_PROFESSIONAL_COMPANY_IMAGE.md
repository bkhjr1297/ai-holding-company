# Professional Company Image Blueprint

Goal: make the business look and operate like a larger professional company while staying lean and agent-staffed.

## External Positioning

Use team-based language truthfully:

- "response desk"
- "client success team"
- "operations team"
- "scheduling desk"
- "reporting team"

Avoid lying about headcount, offices, or humans. We can say "our team" because the business has defined operating roles and agent-staffed functions.

## Suggested Brand Voice

- Calm
- Operationally competent
- Revenue-focused
- Plainspoken
- Local-business friendly
- No AI hype unless it supports the outcome

## Website Sections

1. Hero: "Stop losing jobs to missed calls."
2. Pain: missed calls, slow web-lead follow-up, voicemail drop-offs.
3. Service: we follow up, qualify, book, and report.
4. Proof mechanism: response-time tracking + recovered opportunity report.
5. Offer: 14-day pilot.
6. FAQ: no long contract, integrates with existing phone/calendar, clear reporting.
7. CTA: "Get a missed-revenue audit."
