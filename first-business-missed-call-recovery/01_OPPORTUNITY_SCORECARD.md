## Why #1 Wins

Missed-call recovery/appointment setting has the cleanest first-launch economics:

- Immediate ROI story: one recovered job can pay for the service.
- Easy cold call opener: ask whether missed calls/web leads are being followed up fast.
- Fulfillment can begin manually with a spreadsheet/CRM and scripts.
- Professional company image is easy: present as a dedicated response desk/team.
- Monthly reporting proves value.
- It creates a gateway into additional retainers: reputation management, SEO, ads, web chat, CRM cleanup, and customer support.
