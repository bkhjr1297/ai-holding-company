# First Business Decision: Missed-Call Recovery & Appointment Setting Service

## Decision

Launch the first portfolio business as a **Missed-Call Recovery & Appointment Setting Service** for local home-service businesses.

Working brand placeholder: **Revenue Rescue Desk**.

## Why This First

This business satisfies the CEO Agent charter and Brian's new constraint:

- Remote sellable: yes.
- Monthly retainers: yes.
- Low startup cost: yes, can start with free/open-source tooling and manual/agent-assisted workflows.
- Proven pain: missed calls and slow follow-up cost local service businesses real jobs.
- Simple buyer: owner/operator or office manager.
- Standardized delivery: capture missed inquiries, follow up, book appointments, report saved opportunities.
- Agent-operable: lead follow-up, email/SMS drafts, call scripts, CRM updates, reporting, and customer success can be handled by agents with human approval where required.
- Professional image: can present as a staffed operations desk with sales, scheduling, customer success, reporting, and QA roles.

## Offer Summary

**We help home-service businesses recover missed revenue by following up with missed calls, web inquiries, and quote requests faster — then booking qualified appointments onto their calendar.**

## Beachhead ICP

Initial niche: **local home-service companies** with high-value jobs and urgent inbound demand.

Best first sub-niches:

1. HVAC companies
2. Plumbing companies
3. Roofing companies
4. Garage door companies
5. Pest control companies
6. Electrical contractors

## Primary Buyer

- Owner/operator
- General manager
- Office manager
- Dispatch manager

## Initial Pricing

| Tier | Setup | Monthly | Included |
|---|---:|---:|---|
| Pilot | $0-$199 | $497/mo | Missed-call/web-lead follow-up, CRM tracking, weekly report, up to 50 opportunities/mo |
| Standard | $299 | $997/mo | Up to 150 opportunities/mo, appointment booking, weekly reporting, scripts, response-time tracking |
| Growth | $499 | $1,997/mo | Multi-location or higher volume, custom workflows, monthly optimization call |

Default launch offer: **14-day pilot or first 10 recovered opportunities tracked.**

## Success Metrics

- Reply/contact rate on missed opportunities
- Appointments booked
- Estimated revenue recovered
- Average response time
- Lead leakage reduced
- Client retention and referrals
