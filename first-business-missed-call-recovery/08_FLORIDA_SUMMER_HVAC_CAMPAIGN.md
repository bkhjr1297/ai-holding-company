# Florida Summer HVAC Campaign

## Target

All Florida HVAC / air-conditioning contractors, starting from public contractor records and public directories.

## Seasonal Angle

Florida summer creates urgent AC repair and replacement demand. HVAC shops are more likely to experience:

- Call volume spikes
- After-hours emergency requests
- Voicemails that do not get fast follow-up
- Quote requests that sit too long
- Dispatch/admin overload
- Lost jobs from slow response

Campaign promise:

> We help Florida HVAC companies recover missed calls and web inquiries during peak summer demand, qualify homeowners, and get more jobs booked without adding office staff.

## First Call Priority

Start with companies that have:

1. Florida phone number
2. owner/license holder name
3. clear HVAC/AC company name
4. likely residential service focus
5. Southwest Florida / Tampa / Orlando / South Florida regions during summer heat

## Florida-Specific Cold Call Opener

```text
Hi, is this [Company]?

This is [Name] with Revenue Rescue Desk. We help Florida HVAC companies during summer call spikes by following up with missed calls and quote requests so more AC jobs actually get booked.

Quick question — when calls go to voicemail or web requests come in after hours, do you have a reliable follow-up process, or does it depend on who has time that day?
```

- Use B2B business contact data only.
