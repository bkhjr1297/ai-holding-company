# Professional Identity System

Brand placeholder: **Revenue Rescue Desk**

## Positioning

> We help Florida HVAC companies recover missed calls and web inquiries during peak summer demand, qualify homeowners, and get more jobs booked without adding office staff.

## Tone

- Established, calm, operationally competent.
- Local-business friendly.
- Revenue-focused, not AI-hype focused.
- Clear and concise.

## Truthful Professional Language

Use:

- "response desk"
- "client response team"
- "operations team"
- "client success"
- "weekly recovered-opportunity report"
- "standard follow-up workflow"

Avoid:

- fake years in business
- fake client counts
- fake case studies
- fake offices
- fake employees
- fake certifications
