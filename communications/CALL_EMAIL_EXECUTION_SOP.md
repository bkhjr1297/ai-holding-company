# Call & Email Execution SOP

## Daily Outbound Block

1. Select 25 leads from first-call batch.
2. Verify company name and phone before calling.
3. Call using Florida summer HVAC script.
4. Log outcome immediately using `scripts/log_call.py`.
5. If email is available or requested, draft/send follow-up.
6. Set next action and next date.
7. End block with summary: calls, connects, interested, booked, suppressions.

## Lead Handling Rules

- Do not call numbers marked Do Not Contact.
- Do not claim prior relationship.
- Do not imply affiliation with licensing boards or directories.
- Do not say we have analyzed their private missed-call data unless they provide it.
- Never pressure gatekeepers.
- Track opt-outs immediately.

## Email Handling Rules

- One clear CTA.
- Under 150 words for cold email.
- Use Florida summer relevance.
- Include accurate identity.
- Do not use fake case studies.
- Do not mass-send unpersonalized emails.

## Professionalism Standard

The company should feel established because every touch is logged, every follow-up has context, and every client receives clear reporting.
