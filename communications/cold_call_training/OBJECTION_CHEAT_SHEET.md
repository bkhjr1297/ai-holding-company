# Objection Cheat Sheet

| Prospect Says | You Say |
|---|---|
| Who are you? | Brian with Revenue Rescue Desk. We help HVAC companies recover missed calls and web inquiries during busy summer periods. |
| We already answer our phones. | That is good. The gap is usually overflow, after-hours, or quote forms that do not get fast follow-up. Do you have that covered too? |
| We have an answering service. | That may be perfect. Do they only take messages, or do they also qualify and help book appointments? |
| We are too busy. | That is exactly why I called. During busy weeks, missed calls and slow follow-up can leak jobs. I can be brief — is that handled already? |
| Not interested. | No problem. I appreciate your time. If missed calls become an issue during the summer rush, we are happy to help. |
| Send me info. | Absolutely. What is the best email? And should I focus on missed calls, after-hours requests, or web quote follow-up? |
| How much? | It depends on volume. The pilot is designed so one recovered job can justify it. The first step is a quick audit to see if there is even a leak. |
| Are you local? | We focus on Florida HVAC companies and operate remotely as a response desk. The work is phone, web inquiry, follow-up, and reporting. |
| Is this AI? | We use AI-assisted systems behind the scenes, but the service is simple: faster follow-up, better tracking, and more booked jobs. |
| Call me later. | Sure — what time is best? I will make a note and keep it brief. |
| Take me off your list. | Absolutely. I will mark you do-not-contact. Have a good day. |
