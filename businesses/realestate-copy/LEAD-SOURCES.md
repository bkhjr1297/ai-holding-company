# Lead Sources

## Tier 1 — High intent, immediate
1. **Zillow / Realtor.com "See more" traffic capture.** Agent drives 50–500 clicks/month to a squeeze page offering the listing description + seller prep checklist in exchange for email.
2. **Cold outreach to agents with expired listings.** Pull expired/withdrawn MLS feed per county. 3-touch sequence: hook with comparable sold data → case study → offer free rewrite of one expired listing.
3. **Real estate team leads.** Team leaders managing 5+ agents buy copy in bulk to standardize quality. Outreach via LinkedIn and team-focused Facebook groups.

## Tier 2 — Compounding content
4. **Before/after listing description case studies.** Publish transformed MLS copy with outcome data (days on market, offer count). Syndicate to Medium, LinkedIn, and agent Facebook groups.
5. **Instagram / TikTok short-form content.** "This description got 14 showings in 3 days." Show original copy vs. rewritten copy. High share rate among agents.
6. **Email newsletter for agents.** Weekly "Listing Lab" — one description dissected for wins and losses. CTA at the bottom to outsource the rewrite.

## Tier 3 — Partnerships
7. **Brokerage co-marketing.** Co-host a 30-minute webinar with a brokerage's marketing director. Offer a free team trial to attendees.
8. **Transaction coordinator referrals.** TCs who see agents squinting at listing drafts refer them in exchange for commission or reciprocal listing.
9. **Mortgage broker cross-sell.** Mortgage officers refer new listing agents during listing presentation workshops.

## Tier 4 — Paid channel (only after offer proven)
10. **Facebook/Instagram lead ads targeted to licensed real estate agents.** Interest: NAR, local MLS, real estate coaching. Lead magnet = "The 5-Description Teardown Guide."
11. **Google Search Ads.** Keywords: "listing description service," "MLS copywriter for real estate," "real estate listing writing service."

## Daily activity commitment
- Cold outreach to 30 expired listing agents per day
- 2 pieces of social content per week
- 1 partnership conversation per week
- Weekly review of lead conversion rates; cut channels under 3% response
