## Conversion psychology
- Subscription positioned as "$16/day for unlimited copy" (reduces sticker shock)
- à la carte acts as tripwire — once an agent uses one $29 listing, subscription becomes the natural upgrade
- Team plans priced at ~30% below per-agent solo to win brokerage adoption
- Rental / investment portfolio plan (10+ units/month) available on quote

## Payment terms

- Net-30 invoicing available for brokerages with 5+ transactions/year
