# Positioning

## Brand promise
Listing copy that makes buyers scroll, click, and call.

## Elevator pitch
We write the listing descriptions that make buyers imagine their furniture in the room, their kids in the yard, and their life in your property — before they ever step inside. Turn Zillow browsers into showing appointments with copy engineered to convert.

## Core value equation
```
           Transformation (sold in 12 days vs. 38)
Value = ─────────────────────────────────────────────────
     Time to write (2 minutes of copy review, not 2 hours)
```

## Differentiators
1. **Agent-tested templates.** Trained on 12,000+ top-performing MLS descriptions ranked by CTR, days on market, and offer rate.
2. **Voice calibration.** Copy matches the agent's brand — luxury, family-focused, investor-literal, first-time-buyer-warm.
3. **Same-day delivery.** Most listings back by end of business. Rush +90 minutes for an additional fee.
4. **Compliant by default.** State-specific disclosure language, Fair Housing compliance check built into every output.
5. **Platform-optimized.** MLS fields, Zillow/Realtor.com syndication, Instagram captions, and email teaser copy in one order.

## Target customer profile
- **Primary:** Solo agent doing 12–35 transactions/year, tired of writing the same generic descriptions
- **Secondary:** Team leader buying copy supply for 3–10 agents
- **Tertiary:** Boutique brokerage looking to differentiate their brand through superior listing marketing

## Anti-positioning
We do not do buyer representation copy, transaction coordination, or real estate photography. We are not an AI software tool (your agent learns our tool and leaves). We are a done-for-you copy service wrapped in an AI workforce.
