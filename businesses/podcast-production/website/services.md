## Service Catalog

### Core Production
**What you get:**
- Raw to master file editing
- EQ, compression, noise reduction, de-essing
- Chapter markers (titles + timestamps)
- Mixed music bed + custom outro
- Normalized loudness (-16 LUFS)
- MP3 + WAV deliverables

**Included on plans:** Ignite, Blaze

---

### Show Notes + Copy
**What you get:**
- Episode summary (~300 words, brand-aligned)
- Bullet-point highlights for scrollers
- Key quote pull + timestamp
- SEO-conscious episode title (with your approval)

**Included on plans:** Ignite, Blaze

---

### Transcription
**What you get:**
- Word-level timestamped transcript (.vtt + .docx)
- Human-reviewed for proper nouns + technical terms
- Search-indexed, stored 12 months
- Subtitles-ready format

**Add-on:** $75/episode · **Included:** Blaze

---

### Multi-Platform Distribution
**What you get:**
- RSS feed setup (Libsyn, Buzzsprout, or your host)
- Directory validation + submission (Apple, Spotify, Amazon, Google)
- Platform-specific cover art check
- Scheduled publish dates
- Post-publish confirmation report

**Included on plans:** Ignite, Blaze

**Add-on:** $450/episode

**Included on plans:** Ignite, Blaze

## Process Map

```
Record → Upload → Edit Queue → Review → Publish → Report
  1h     24h       48h        48h     48h      Monthly
```

**Typical Ignite timeline:**

00:00 — You upload raw audio
06:00 — Assigned to senior editor
12:00 — First cut ready for QA
24:00 — Mixed + mastered
36:00 — Show notes drafted
48:00 — Your review + final approval
72:00 — Published + confirmation sent

Blaze: same flow with 24hr SLA.
