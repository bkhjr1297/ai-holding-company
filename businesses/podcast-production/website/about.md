## Our Operating Principles

**1. Frictionless handoffs**
We give you a shared folder with clear structure. You drop files. Result appears. No ports to map.

**2. Standards over style**
We establish a production standard that becomes your brand's audio fingerprint. Consistent sound strengthens recall.

**3. Platform discipline**
Distribution isn't an afterthought. Each platform's requirements get checked before we ship.

**4. Security + ownership**
You keep access to source files and RSS ownership. We're prepress, not publisher.

**5. Radical transparency**
Monthly report, loudness print, edit log, platform health. No black boxes.
