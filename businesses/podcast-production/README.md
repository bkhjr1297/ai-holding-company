## How to Use This Kit

1. **Review pricing** — validate against competitor benchmarks.
2. **Publish website copy** — plug into CMS; swap in your domain + contact info.
3. **Deploy order form** — integrate with Typeform/Tally, route to intake folder watched by `intake_router_stub.py`.
4. **Configure templates** — sync Episode Tracker to Notion/Supabase.
5. **Adopt SOP** — assign PMs to full-cycle SOP by onboarding week 1.
6. **Run automation** — start in dry-run mode; test with synthetic intake forms.
7. **Launch lead campaigns** — begin partner outreach; activate SEO pages.
