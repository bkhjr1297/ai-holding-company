## Package Descriptions

### Spark — _Validate your format, cheaply_
For creators testing the waters. We keep it tight: high signal, minimal polish, enough for an RSS-ready feed. Use solo content; remote interviews require the Ignite plan.

### Ignite — _Your daily driver_
For active shows that want to look pro without hiring an engineer. Includes proactive PM, polished cuts with chapter markers, curated show notes, and we submit to directories for you.

### Blaze — _Show as product line 1_
For brands or serial creators who need a production team on standby. SlAs, weekly analytics, 24hr turnaround. You own every raw file if you leave.
