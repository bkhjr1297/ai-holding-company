# Lead Sources — AI Resume Writing Agency

## Tier 1: Paid Acquisition
1. LinkedIn Lead Gen Forms (targeting "Job Seeker" + "Recently Laid Off" audiences).
2. Google SEM: "AI resume builder", "resume rewrite service", "ATS resume check".
3. Reddit /dev/retire, /resumes, /cscareerquestions sponsored posts.
4. TikTok/Reels cuts: "I rebuilt my resume with AI and got 4 interviews in 2 days".

## Tier 2: Partnerships
5. Career coaches: white-label referral code, 30% rev share.
6. Bootcamp alumni networks (General Assembly, Springboard).
7. University career centers (executive resume add-on).
8. Recruiting agencies (referral for their candidates).

## Tier 3: Organic / Content
9. Free ATS checker landing page → capture email → drip sequence.
10. "Resume Roast" newsletter (weekly Friday edition, public roast of anonymized bad bullet points).
11. YouTube shorts: "Before/After AI resume transformations".
12. LinkedIn posts: daily resume tip carousels, job search macro commentary.

## Tier 4: Outbound
13. Scrape LinkedIn profiles with recent "Open to Work" banner → personalized DM sequence.
14. Mass layoff tracking (API: layoffs.fyi) → outreach to newly unemployed tech workers.
15. Follow up job postings on Indeed: "Hey, I spotted your posting. Want a free resume fit report?"

## Lead Magnet
- Free 1-page ATS Fit Report: upload resume, get a score and top 3 fixes.
- This is the primary conversion point for paid.

## Metrics to Watch
- CPL (cost per lead): target < $4.
- Lead-to-customer rate: target ≥ 25%.
- Referral rate: target ≥ 15%.
- Drip email open rate: target ≥ 22%.
