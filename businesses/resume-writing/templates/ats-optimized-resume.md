# Resume Template — ATS Optimized

## Structure Rules
- Use standard section headers: `Professional Experience`, `Education`, `Skills`, `Projects`.
- No text boxes, images, tables, or columns in the body—ATS can't read them.
- Bullet format only for experience bullets.
- Font: Calibri, Roboto, or Helvetica 10.5–11pt.
- Margins: 0.6–0.75 inches.
