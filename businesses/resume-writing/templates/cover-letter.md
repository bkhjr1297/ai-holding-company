# Cover Letter Template

## Structure
- 3–4 short paragraphs, max 250 words.
- Match tone to industry (formal for finance, punchy for startups).
- 1 story → 1 skill → 1 result pattern.

---

## Template

**Dear [Hiring Manager],**

I’m excited to apply for the [Role] position at [Company]. With [X] years of [expertise] and a track record of [result], I’m confident I can help your team hit [specific company goal].

At [Current Organization], I led [project/initiative] that [quantifiable outcome]. This experience directly maps to the [specific responsibility] outlined in your posting—particularly the emphasis on [keyword from JD].

I’m drawn to [Company] because [specific value/proud moment]. I’d love to bring my [skill set] and [soft strength] to help [team/department] succeed.

Let’s schedule a quick call to discuss how my background aligns with your needs.

**Best,**
[Your Name]
