# 15-Minute Discovery Script

## Opener
Thanks for taking the time. I reviewed the public website and found a few places where AI-assisted buyers may not clearly understand your services, service area, proof, or quote path.

## Questions
1. Which service do you most want more jobs for?
2. Which cities/areas matter most?
3. Are most leads phone calls, forms, or referrals?
4. Do customers ask the same questions before booking?
5. Do you already update service pages or FAQs monthly?

## Positioning
The snapshot is not a ranking promise. It is a practical clarity and task-flow review so humans, search engines, and AI assistants can understand the business faster.
