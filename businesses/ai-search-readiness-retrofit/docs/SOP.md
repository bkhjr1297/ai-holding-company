# Fulfillment SOP

## Inputs
- Public website URL
- Business name
- Industry/category
- Primary service area if visible

## Process
1. Open the public website.
2. Identify services in under 60 seconds.
3. Identify service areas in under 60 seconds.
4. Check homepage, service pages, about/proof, FAQ, contact/quote path.
5. Score each dimension 0-5.
6. Capture evidence snippets.
7. Write top 5 fixes.
8. Generate Snapshot report.
9. QA for truthfulness: no ranking claims, no fabricated results.

## QA checklist
- Every finding references public evidence.
- No private data required.
- No contact forms submitted.
- No claims that Google/AI will rank/cite the site.
- Recommendations are specific enough to implement.
