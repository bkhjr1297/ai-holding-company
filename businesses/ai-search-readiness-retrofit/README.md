# AI Search Visibility & Agent-Readable Content Retrofit

A zero-startup-cost service business that helps local service companies make their websites easier for Google AI Search, answer engines, and browser/AI agents to understand.

## Core offer

**AI Search Readiness Snapshot** — a public-website-only review that scores whether an AI-assisted buyer can understand what the company does, where it serves, why to trust it, and how to request service.

## Target buyers
- HVAC companies
- Plumbing companies
- Roofing companies
- Dental/medical clinics with public websites
- Home-service contractors with weak service pages

## Revenue model
- Free/low-friction diagnostic preview
- $299-$799 one-time retrofit plan
- $300-$900/month monitoring + content refresh retainer

## Compliance / safety
- Public website review only.
- No client logins required for the snapshot.
- No guarantees of ranking, citation, or AI placement.
- No outreach/calls/emails are sent automatically.
- Recommendations are truthful and evidence-based.
