# Compliance Rules For All Three Models

## Cold Email Rules
- Real name, real company identity.
- Relevant, targeted outreach only.
- Include clear opt-out language.
- Honor opt-outs immediately.
- No deceptive headers or subject lines.
- Do not use non-permissioned data.

## Cold Calling Rules
- B2B manual cold calling: generally legal during legal hours.
- Call 8am-9pm local recipient time.
- Do-not-contact requests honored immediately.
- No prerecorded or AI-generated voice for cold outbound.
- No caller ID spoofing or number rotation.

## Data Rules
- Public, free, or permissioned data only.
- Do not bypass anti-abuse or KYC systems.
- Do not reuse contacts who request removal.

## Identity
We operate as Revenue Rescue Desk.
All outreach is truthful, professional, and value-focused.
