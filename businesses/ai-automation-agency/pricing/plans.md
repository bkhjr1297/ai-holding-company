# Pricing

## Implementation Packages

### Starter Automation
- **Price:** $2,500
- **Includes:** 1 core workflow, up to 3 integrations, basic QA and launch support
- **Best for:** Lead response, scheduling, or document processing

### Growth Automation
- **Price:** $7,500
- **Includes:** 2–3 workflows, up to 6 integrations, prompt pack, reporting
- **Best for:** Support + nurture, CRM cleanup, multi-channel campaigns

### Enterprise Automation
- **Price:** starts at $15,000
- **Includes:** multi-department workflow map, custom integrations, dedicated PM, transition to managed service
- **Best for:** succession planning, growth-stage ops teams, agencies

## Managed Retainers

| Tier | Price | Coverage |

| Starter | $750/month | 2 automations, maintenance, weekly health checks |
| Growth | $1,995/month | 4 automations, weekly reporting, optimization sprints |

## One-time Add-ons
- Voice AI phone assistant setup: $1,500
- Internal knowledge base / RAG setup: $2,000
- Custom integration build: $1,000–$3,000 per integration
- Recurring optimization sprint: $1,500 per sprint

## Payment terms
- Implementation: 50% start, 50% usable preview
- Retainer: monthly auto-pay
- Add-ons: prepay or included in larger scope if scoped in advance
